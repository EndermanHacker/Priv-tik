Privacy Policy

We value your privacy. This Policy explains what information we collect and how we use it.

1. Information We Collect

  • Authentication data: handled via TikTok OAuth. We do not see or store your TikTok password.
  • Tokens: Access/refresh tokens may be stored securely on your device/server to enable posting on your behalf.
  • Usage data: Non-personal analytics (e.g., error logs) to improve the app’s reliability.

2. How We Use Information

  • To connect your TikTok account and post content you request.
  • To maintain and improve the app’s functionality and security.

3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell your data. We only share information with service providers as necessary to operate the app, or when required by law.

4. Data Security

We use reasonable technical and organizational measures to protect tokens and any stored data.

5. Your Choices

  • You may disconnect the app from TikTok at any time via TikTok account settings.
  • You may delete stored tokens by uninstalling the app or clearing its data.

6. Children’s Privacy

The app is not directed to children under the age permitted by TikTok’s policies.

7. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy. Continued use after updates constitutes acceptance.

8. Contact

If you have questions about this Policy, please contact us.
